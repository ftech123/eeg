Carpal Tunnel Syndrome Symptom Checker

Recommendations:

  • Rest: Avoid repetitive movements and take frequent breaks.
  • Stretching: Perform wrist and hand stretches regularly to relieve tension.
  • Ergonomics: Ensure proper ergonomics at workstations to reduce strain on the wrists.

Note: This symptom checker provides general information and is not a diagnostic tool. Consult a healthcare professional for proper evaluation and diagnosis.
