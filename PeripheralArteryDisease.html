Peripheral Artery Disease Symptom Checker

Recommendations:

  • Quit smoking: Smoking can worsen symptoms of PAD.
  • Exercise: Regular physical activity can improve circulation and reduce symptoms.
  • Healthy diet: Maintain a diet low in saturated fat, cholesterol, and sodium.
