Type 2 Diabetes Symptom Checker

Recommendations:

  • Healthy diet: Eat a balanced diet low in refined sugars and carbohydrates.
  • Regular exercise: Engage in physical activity most days of the week.
  • Monitor blood sugar: Keep track of blood glucose levels as recommended by a healthcare professional.

Note: This symptom checker provides general information and is not a diagnostic tool. Consult a healthcare professional for proper evaluation and diagnosis.
