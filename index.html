Geriatric Assist

Telecare using EEG and Kinect sensor for elderly and disabled people


Geriatric Assist is a state-of-the-art telecare solution designed to cater to the unique needs of elderly and disabled individuals. By harnessing the power of EEG and Kinect sensors, it offers personalized assistance and round-the-clock monitoring. The EEG technology monitors brain activity, enabling early detection of cognitive changes or health issues, while the Kinect sensor tracks movement and activity, ensuring the user's safety. This innovative system promotes independence and peace of mind for both users and caregivers, revolutionizing the way telecare is delivered.
